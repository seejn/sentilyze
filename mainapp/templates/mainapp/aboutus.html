About us

Sentilyze is a sentiment analysis website that specializes in analyzing customer reviews and comments. Sentylize provides an interface for merchants to advertise their products and interact with other merchants. Sentylize is designed to work as a service provider for merchants, providing them with valuable insights into their customers' thoughts and opinions.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly interface for merchants so that they can have easily generate sentiment analysis reports. We provide merchants a platform for them to identify the trends in customer sentiment over time. Merchants can make data-driven decisions to improve their bottom line with the help of streamlining reporting process and configurable reports.
